Let's reclaim our space dream!


Maskerad 2021

A lot happened in the 1960s. Wars and walls and tension, but also lava lamps, fighting for your rights, the Beatles and dancing barefoot. In the meantime, here in Finland, TOKYO was established in the Institute of Industrial Arts in 1961. In that sense, we come from that generation of dreamers. People were even singing that "All you Have To Do is Dream" back then. They were dreaming about peace, love, freedom, and, with an immense excitement, goosebumps and tears in the corner of their eyes, people were dreaming about space.

Isn't it so ironic, how 60 years have passed, and the space race is back again, but this time with a bitter taste of privilege, elitism and exessive amount of CO2. Is it just a party for elons and jeffs, or are we still invited?

It's time to reclaim our space dream. We want to bring back the excitement about the unknown and unexplored, which can make a heart skip a beat. We want to dream as people all over the planet dreamt 60 years ago. Hereby, we officially announce the theme for the long awaited TOKYO Maskerad 2021: DIY Space Dream.
