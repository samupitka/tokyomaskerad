Our Paradise in Under Construction


There are as many paradises as there are people. The background of the word is biblical, but its meaning has changed throughout the years to mean a more secular, utopy-like place. However, the paradise imagined is always being transformed and its meanings change.

Paradise is often called the holiest place, where the eternal happies and delight reside. Eternity might be lived there. Homo Sapiens lives there in balance with the rest of the nature.

Everything is always under construction. Cities, Otaniemi Campus, our own lives and identities, our friendships and romances, our hopes and fears, our future and relationship with the past. How we see history and how we imagine future are under construction.

We can imagine our own paradises and use our best of knowledge and skills to make the world we live in into a paradise. Until then, it's under construction.
